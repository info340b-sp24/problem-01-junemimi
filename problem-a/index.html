Snoopy

Snoopy is a beagle in the comic strip and television show series Peanuts . He is a loyal, fun friend who loves to play imaginative fantasy games. In his fantasy games, he lives different lives such as an author, college student, attorney, and a World War I flying ace.

Reasons why I admire Snoopy:

  • He is very loving and takes care of his friends
  • He has a great imagination
  • He is a talented dancer!
